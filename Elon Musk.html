Elon Musk is a South African-born American entrepreneur and investor who co-founded the electronic-payment firm PayPal and formed SpaceX, maker of launch vehicles and spacecraft. He was also one of the first significant investors in, as well as chief executive officer of, the electric car manufacturer Tesla. In addition, Musk acquired Twitter (later X) in 2022.

SpaceX is a spaceflight company founded by Elon Musk in 2002. It aims to make humans a multi-planetary species by colonizing Mars.

Tesla is an electric car manufacturer founded by Elon Musk in 2003. It produces high-performance and environmentally friendly vehicles that run on batteries.

Twitter is a social media platform acquired by Elon Musk in 2022. It allows users to post short messages called tweets and interact with other users.
